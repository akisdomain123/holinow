About Holi

Holi is a Hindu festival that celebrates the arrival of spring, love, and the victory of good over evil. It is known as the Festival of Colors.

Why is Holi Celebrated?

Holi is celebrated to mark the victory of good over evil, inspired by the legend of Prahlad and Holika. It also signifies the arrival of spring and the end of winter.

How to Play Holi?

Holi is played by throwing colored powders, splashing water, dancing, and enjoying festive sweets and drinks with family and friends.
